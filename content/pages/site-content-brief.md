## SPLIT SYSTEM AIRCON CONTENT

Split-type air conditioners in Australia refer to a popular type of air conditioning system designed for cooling and heating individual rooms or specific areas. They are called "split" because they consist of two main components: an indoor unit and an outdoor unit, connected by refrigerant piping.

### How Split-Type Air Conditioners Work:
1. **Indoor Unit**: Mounted inside the room, contains the evaporator coil and blower fan. Blows cooled or heated air into the room.
2. **Outdoor Unit**: Installed outside the building, houses the compressor, condenser coil, and fan. Dissipates heat extracted from indoor air.
3. **Refrigerant Piping**: Copper pipes connect indoor and outdoor units, circulating refrigerant to transfer heat.

### Installation Types:

#### 1. BACK-TO-BACK INSTALLATION
Standard installation for single-storey: max 3m copper pipes between indoor and outdoor unit. Two-storey: max 4m. Additional distance: $50/meter for ducting, copper, and electrical work.

#### 2. SIDE ENTRY INSTALLATION
Refrigerant lines, electrical wiring, and drainage run through the side wall instead of straight through behind the indoor unit. Useful for aesthetics or when direct path is obstructed. Price difference: ~$100-$150 extra from back-to-back.

#### 3. UP & OVER INSTALLATION
Pipes run up over a structure (wall/ceiling), then down to outdoor unit. Used when direct drilling isn't feasible or for concealed installation. May require condensation pump ($250+ extra). Price difference: $50-$80 per meter.

### Electrical Work for Aircons:
- **Option 1**: Use power from nearby existing RCD protected circuit (2.5kW–3.5kW units)
- **Option 2**: New safety switch and circuit from unit to switchboard ($250–$550, confirmed on-site). For 5kW–8kW units. Single level, easy roof access, tile roof, up to 20m power line distance.

## DUCTED AIR CONDITIONING CONTENT

Ducted air conditioning: HVAC system that cools/heats an entire home through a network of ducts.

**Benefits:**
- Consistent cooling/heating throughout entire building
- Aesthetic appeal — hidden ducts and discreet vents
- Zoning capabilities — different rooms at different temperatures
- Quiet operation — central unit placement reduces noise
- Energy efficient — advanced inverter technology

**Zone Controller:** AirTouch 5 — climate control from touch screen, phone or tablet. https://www.airtouch.net.au/
